Getting Started!

As a first experience in coding, we're going to learn the basics of HTML and CSS. These are the two languages used to create webpages. Whether you're a pro, or you've never coded before, this is a great place to jump in and actually do coding! As an added bonus, you don't need install anything on your computer to get started. Why HTML/CSS? Because webpages aren't actually programs. You'll be learning the basic idea of how we give a computer commands - how we teach a computer to do things without the added complexity of creating a full program. What should you take away from this?

A computer will always do EXACTLY what you ask it, even when you ask it to do something other than what you intended How to break out a complicated idea into simple steps that can be followed in order to achieve a desired outcome The importance of syntax - that formatting in computer code is critical That you only need to memorize a FEW commands. It's much more effective to know about the basic ideas and then search the web for what you need as you write your code.
